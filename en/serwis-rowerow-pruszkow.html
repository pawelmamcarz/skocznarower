It is a 15 minute drive from Pruszków to my workshop. I repair dirt jump bikes, BMX, MTB, city bikes and the mechanical side of e-bikes. Riders from Pruszków itself also come here looking for someone who trues a dirt jump or BMX wheel with millimetre precision, not by eye.

Why the trip from Pruszków is worth it

Pruszków is the largest town in the area and has several bike shops, but most of them mainly handle city and trekking bikes, the kind people ride to the WKD or PKP station. If you own a dirt jump bike, BMX, MTB or anything that needs precision (wheel building, brake bleeding, derailleur hanger straightening), it is hard to find a workshop in Pruszków itself that understands this specialty. Customers reach me from Ostoja, Żbików, Gąsin and the town centre.

A few specifics before you book:

  • Specialist tools. Professional truing stand with a runout dial indicator, torque wrench, hydraulic bleed kit, derailleur hanger alignment tool. Every part of the repair is done with the right tool, no improvising.
  • Dirt jump and BMX experience. I ride the same kind of bikes myself, so I know what a badly set up bike means on the track. If you ride skateparks or the local dirt jumps, your bike is serviced by someone who has felt it in his own ankles.
  • Straightforward pricing. The rates are on the home page. After an inspection I tell you what actually needs doing and what it will cost, before I unscrew anything.

FAQ: Pruszków

How long is the drive from Pruszków?

About 15 minutes by car via DK719. In rush hour it can be 20 minutes, but on weekends and in the evening it is an easy 12-15 minutes.

Can I bring my bike without an appointment?

No. I work alone and by appointment only. First book a date online, I will call you back and we will settle the day and time. That way I have the time to work on your bike properly, not between other visits.

Do you pick up bikes from Pruszków?

Yes, from 50 zł. When booking, choose the "Bike pick-up and delivery" service and put the address and an approximate pick-up time in the note.

Do you service an e-bike bought in Pruszków?

I service the mechanical side of any e-bike: brakes, wheels, components. Electronics and the motor stay with authorised service centres. If your e-bike has a mechanical problem, I can help.

How much is a bike tune-up for a customer from Pruszków?

A basic tune-up starts at 150 zł, a comprehensive one at 340 zł. Prices are the same regardless of where you live. The full price list is on the home page.

Do you service brand bikes (Trek, Specialized, Canyon, YT)?

Yes, I service any brand: Trek, Specialized, Canyon, YT and others. What matters is not the frame brand but the specific components on the bike. Shimano, SRAM, TRP, Hope or Magura brakes get bled with the procedure of the given manufacturer, no matter what is written on the frame. The exception is warranty repairs, those stay with the official dealer, everything else comes to me as usual.
