What sets this workshop apart

What is behind it:

  • Professional truing stand with a dial indicator. Most workshops true wheels "by eye". My truing stand has a dial indicator measuring runout to tenths of a millimetre. For dirt jump, BMX or MTB wheels, which take hard hits, that makes a real difference in durability.
  • Hydraulic brake bleeding. I bleed Shimano, SRAM, TRP, Hope and Magura brakes. A full toolkit for bleeding, oil changes and bite-point adjustment. Priced from 100 zł per circuit.
  • DJ/BMX specialty. I ride dirt jump and BMX myself, these bikes have been with me since primary school. If you jump in a skatepark, your bike lands with someone who knows why your crank keeps coming loose.

FAQ: Milanówek

How fast can you get here from Milanówek?

From most of Milanówek 1-3 minutes by car, up to 10 minutes by bike.

Can I ride my own bike over even with a problem?

Yes, the distance is short enough that even a bike with a loose brake or a wobbly pedal will make it. Just ride carefully. If the bike does not ride at all, pick the "Bike pick-up and drop-off" service when booking.

Do you service electric bikes bought in Milanówek?

Yes, the mechanical side of any e-bike. Brakes, wheels, components. I do not touch the electronics or the motor, those belong with the manufacturer's authorised service centres.

How do I book a visit?

The fastest way is the online form. You pick a service, a day and a time, and I call back to confirm. You can also call (600 370 810) or write on WhatsApp.

How much does a bike tune-up cost for a customer from Milanówek?

A basic tune-up starts from 150 zł, a full overhaul from 340 zł. Prices are the same no matter where you ride in from. The full price list is on the homepage.

Do you work on brand-name bikes (Trek, Specialized, Canyon, YT)?

Yes, the frame brand plays no role here, the components do. Trek, Specialized, Canyon or YT, it makes no difference: I bleed Shimano, SRAM, TRP, Hope or Magura brakes following the given component maker's procedure, and wheels and drivetrains get serviced the same way. One caveat: I do not handle warranty repairs, those stay with the authorised dealer. Anything outside warranty is business as usual.
